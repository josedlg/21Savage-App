Aircraft Health Monitoring- Aircraft Maintenance Reduction Opportunity

Systems on aircraft tend to fail over time and can cause tremendous delays and cancellations if not taken care of before a total system failure. AeroClient uses modern aircraft manufacturer software to keep track of aircrafts and their respective systems to optimize efficiency of aircraft. In addition to our aircraft specific health monitoring software, AeroClient will review technical records from the crew & maintenance and issue action on any repetitive items. A repetitive item is defined as a specific system failure that does not correct after multiple repair attempts. By monitoring system health and technical interruptions AeroClient is able to provide Technical Service Orders (TSO) which will guide maintenance personnel with work steps to repair ongoing defects. Using this service will reduce man hour cost, possible AOG/delays and provide you with a healthier fleet.
